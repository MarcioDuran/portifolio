Design System

The Ysense UX Design System consists of the following key components: Design Guidelines: Clear and comprehensive guidelines that ensure consistency and coherence across all Ysense products and services. UI Components: A library of reusable UI components, such as buttons, forms, and navigation menus, that can be easily implemented in various projects. Visual Assets: A collection of visual assets, including icons, illustrations, and images, that maintain a consistent visual style throughout Ysense's digital presence. Accessibility Standards: A set of accessibility standards and best practices to ensure that Ysense's products and services are accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities. Design Tools: A curated selection of design tools and software that help Ysense's designers and developers work more efficiently and effectively. Collaboration and Communication: A system for effective collaboration and communication between designers, developers, and other stakeholders, ensuring that everyone is aligned and working towards the same goals. Continuous Improvement: A process for regularly reviewing and updating the UX Design System to incorporate new insights, technologies, and user feedback, ensuring that Ysense's products and services are always evolving to meet the needs of its users.

Ysense and Zazzle are two companies that have successfully implemented the Double Diamond design process to develop innovative solutions and creative ideas . Develop: Ysense and Zazzle used the Double Diamond process to develop a range of solutions to the problem. They brainstormed ideas and created prototypes to test with users . Deliver: After testing the prototypes, Ysense and Zazzle delivered the final solution that met the users' needs and solved the problem . By using the Double Diamond process, Ysense and Zazzle were able to create a user-centered solution that improved the website's user experience and met the users' needs.

. The Double Diamond process is a framework that allows companies to apply design characteristics to find solutions to complex problems

. The process involves four stages: Discover, Define, Develop, and Deliver

. Here's how Ysense and Zazzle used the Double Diamond process to solve a problem:

Discover: Ysense and Zazzle identified a problem with their website's user experience. They conducted research to understand the users' needs and pain points

Define: Based on the research, Ysense and Zazzle defined the problem and set clear goals for the project
