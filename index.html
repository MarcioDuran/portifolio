UX Case Zazzle

Zazzle is an online marketplace and custom print-on-demand company that allows designers and artists to create and sell a wide range of personalized products. The platform enables users to design and customize items such as clothing, accessories, home decor, stationery, and more. Customers can either purchase ready-made products or personalize them by adding their designs, text, or images. Zazzle's business model involves printing and shipping custom-designed products to customers on behalf of designers and store owners. It is a platform that brings together designers, sellers, and buyers, making it a popular choice for creative entrepreneurs and those looking for unique, personalized products.

Information architecture

Ysense, also known as ClixSense, was a platform that primarily focused on paid online surveys and other micro-tasks that allowed users to earn money online. Users could sign up for free and participate in surveys and other online activities to earn rewards or cash. Ysense provided opportunities for individuals to make some additional income by offering their opinions and completing tasks from various companies and industries. It acted as a middleman, connecting users with businesses seeking market research data or specific tasks to be completed.
